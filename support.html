Frequently asked

Do my videos get uploaded?

No. All editing, grading, and export happen entirely on your device. Your media never leaves your phone.

Can I use my own LUTs?

Yes. RealLUT imports standard Adobe/Resolve .cube files. Open My LUTs and tap Import to bring in your own looks; they're applied to both preview and export.

What export options are supported?

Resolutions up to 8K, frame rates up to 60 fps, HEVC / H.264 / ProRes codecs, and Rec. 709 / Rec. 2020 / Display P3 color tags. H.264 is capped at 4K by the format itself.

Where did my project go?

RealLUT autosaves your session as a draft when the app goes to the background. Open the side menu → Drafts to reopen or delete saved sessions.
