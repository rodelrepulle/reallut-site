Deleting your data

RealLUT runs on your device — your videos, edits, drafts, and LUTs are stored locally and never uploaded. If you create an optional account (Apple, Google, Facebook, or email) to sync your Pro subscription, the only data stored is your account identifier, sign-in email, and subscription status.

To delete your account and all synced data:

  1. Open RealLUT and go to Profile.
  2. Tap Delete Account and confirm.
  3. Your account and synced entitlement data are permanently removed. Local drafts and LUTs stay on your device and are removed if you delete the app.

If you signed in with Facebook, or no longer have the app installed, email 13jayr37@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my RealLUT data" and we'll remove your account data within 30 days.

Frequently asked

Do my videos get uploaded?

No. All editing, grading, and export happen entirely on your device. Your media never leaves your phone.

Can I use my own LUTs?

Yes. RealLUT imports standard Adobe/Resolve .cube files. Open My LUTs and tap Import to bring in your own looks; they're applied to both preview and export.

What export options are supported?

Resolutions up to 4K Ultra HD, frame rates up to 60 fps, HEVC / H.264 / ProRes codecs, and Rec. 709 / Rec. 2020 / Display P3 color tags.

Where did my project go?

RealLUT autosaves your session as a draft when the app goes to the background. Open the side menu → Drafts to reopen or delete saved sessions.
