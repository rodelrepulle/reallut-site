Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

RealLUT is designed so your footage never leaves your device.

Your videos and photos

Videos and photos you import are processed on your device only. Exported videos are saved to your photo library with your permission. Your media is never uploaded, analyzed remotely, or shared.

LUT files

.cube files you import are stored in the app's local documents folder on your device and are never uploaded anywhere.

Optional account

You can use every editing feature without an account. If you choose to sign in (with Apple, Google, Facebook, or email) to sync a RealLUT Pro subscription across your devices, we store your account identifier, the email address associated with your sign-in method, and your subscription status on our backend provider (Supabase). This data is used solely to operate your account and entitlement sync — never for advertising or tracking, and it is not sold or shared with third parties.

Subscriptions

Purchases are processed by Apple. We receive Apple-signed purchase information (not your payment details) to verify and sync your Pro entitlement.

Account deletion

You can permanently delete your account and synced entitlement data at any time from Profile → Delete Account inside the app.

Analytics and tracking

RealLUT contains no analytics, no advertising frameworks, and no trackers.

Permissions

RealLUT requests photo library access solely to import clips and save your exports. You can revoke this at any time in iOS Settings.

Children

RealLUT is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

If a future version of RealLUT changes how data is handled, this policy will be updated before those changes ship.
