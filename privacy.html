Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

RealLUT is built privacy-first. Everything the app does — importing, color grading, editing, and exporting your videos — happens entirely on your device. We collect no data at all.

Your media never leaves your device

All editing, LUT color grading, adjustments, and export run 100% on-device. Videos you import are copied into RealLUT's private app storage so your drafts keep working offline and survive the original being deleted. We never upload, transmit, or have access to your media.

No accounts

RealLUT does not require — or offer — an account. There is nothing to sign up for and no personal information to provide.

No data collection

RealLUT collects no analytics, no usage data, no identifiers, and no personal information. The app makes no network requests with your content or your data.

No third-party tracking or ads

We do not use advertising SDKs, we do not sell or share any data, and we do not track you across apps or websites.

Children

RealLUT is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Your rights

Because all of your content lives on your device, you control it directly — deleting a draft or deleting the app removes its data completely.
